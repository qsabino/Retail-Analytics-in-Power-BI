# Retail Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This project is an end-to-end **Retail Analytics dashboard** built in Power BI to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends.  
It demonstrates how raw retail data can be transformed into **actionable business insights** through data modeling and visualization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track key metrics: **Revenue, Profit, Orders**
- Identify **top-performing products and customers**
- Analyze **sales trends over time and across regions**
- Enable interactive exploration for business users

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** (Data modeling, visualization)
- **DAX** (Calculated measures & KPIs)
- **Excel / CSV** (Data source & preprocessing)

---

## Dashboard Highlights
- **Sales Overview:** Revenue and order trends over time  
- **Product Analysis:** Top & bottom-performing products  
- **Customer Insights:** High-value customers and contribution  
- **Regional Performance:** Sales comparison by geography  
- **Interactive Filters:** Drill-down by date, category, and region  

---

## Key Insights
- Sales show clear **seasonal patterns and peak periods**  
- A small group of customers contributes a **large share of revenue**  
- Certain products consistently underperform, impacting profitability  

---

## Business Value
This dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Make **data-driven decisions** on sales and inventory  
- Focus on **high-performing segments**  
- Improve overall **profitability and efficiency**
